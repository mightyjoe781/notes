# Books

*Essential reading for system design and distributed systems.*

---

## Core System Design

| Book | Author | Focus |
|------|--------|-------|
| **System Design Interview** (Vol. 1 & 2) | Alex Xu | Interview prep, practical patterns |
| **Designing Data-Intensive Applications** | Martin Kleppmann | Internals of distributed data systems |
| **Building Microservices** (2nd ed.) | Sam Newman | Microservice architecture and practices |
| **The Art of Scalability** | Abbott & Fisher | Organizational and technical scaling |
| **Database Systems: The Complete Book** | Garcia-Molina et al. | Database theory and implementation |

## Reliability & Operations

| Book | Author | Focus |
|------|--------|-------|
| **Site Reliability Engineering** | Google SRE Team | Running production systems at scale |
| **The SRE Workbook** | Google SRE Team | Practical SRE implementations |
| **Release It!** (2nd ed.) | Michael Nygard | Stability patterns, circuit breakers, bulkheads |
| **High Performance MySQL** | Schwartz, Zaitsev, Tkachenko | MySQL internals and optimization |
| **The Phoenix Project** | Kim, Behr, Spafford | DevOps culture and practices (narrative) |

## Reading Order

For interview preparation:

1. *System Design Interview* Vol. 1 (Alex Xu) - start here
2. *Designing Data-Intensive Applications* (Kleppmann) - deep understanding
3. *Building Microservices* (Newman) - microservice patterns
4. *System Design Interview* Vol. 2 (Alex Xu) - advanced problems

For production systems:

1. *Release It!* - stability and resilience patterns
2. *Site Reliability Engineering* - operational practices
3. *The Art of Scalability* - organizational scaling
